Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

Ledgerix Pro LLC ("Ledgerix Pro," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information about you when you use our website ledgerixpro.com and our bookkeeping and financial operations services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company name
  • Business financial diagnostic information you provide through our intake forms
  • SMS consent records (the date and method you opted in to text messages)
  • Usage data and analytics (pages visited, interactions, device and browser metadata)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide bookkeeping and financial operations services
  • To send SMS messages you have consented to receive
  • To contact you about your account, services, and support requests
  • To improve our services, models, and customer experience

3. SMS Communications

  • We only send SMS messages to contacts who have explicitly opted in
  • Message frequency varies based on account activity
  • Msg & data rates may apply
  • To opt out reply STOP to any message
  • To get help reply HELP
  • SMS opt-in data is never shared with third parties

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use standard web analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. This may include cookies and similar tracking technologies. You can control cookie settings through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support of services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We may share information with service providers (such as QuickBooks, Xero, and GoHighLevel) strictly to provide our services to you
  • We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes

6. Data Security

  • Industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure token storage for third-party integrations
  • Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis

We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption for stored tokens, HTTPS for all data transmission, and restricted employee access to sensitive data.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP
  • Contact us with privacy questions at scott@ledgerixpro.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website prior to the change taking effect.
